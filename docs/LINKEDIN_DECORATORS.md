3. **About** — lead with "Red Team Kitchen · Alexios Bluff Mara LLC · building predicted-fMRI + Gemma." Don't lead with "alumnus of X" — lead with the product.
4. **Experience** — "Founder, Alexios Bluff Mara LLC" with 3 bullets on what you've shipped.

## What NOT to bother with

- Coursera "verified" certs that cost $49/course unless tied to a Google/Meta program — low signal vs cost
- LinkedIn Learning paid certs (unless free via library card — CPL gives it free)
- Udemy certificates (no LinkedIn recognition, weak signal)
- Microsoft certs (unless pivoting to Azure — you're all-Google)
