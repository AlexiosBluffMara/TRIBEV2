## What to attach

- PDF export of `docs/ISU_PROPOSAL.md` (one page, clear)
- Optional: 90-second demo video URL once recorded

## Why this framing works

1. **Specific ask, low friction** — "sponsor a guest account" is a well-known 5-minute favor, not an advisor commitment.
2. **Reciprocity built in** — co-authorship consideration is real value for faculty.
3. **Cites their work** — shows you did research, not a form-letter.
4. **Leaves room for escalation** — if they like it, Tier 2/3 partnership (per `ISU_PROPOSAL.md`) is the natural next step.
5. **No aggressive ask** — no money request, no IRB ask upfront.
